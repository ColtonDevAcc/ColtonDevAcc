### About Me

I'm a software architect and entrepreneur with **9 years of experience** building scalable applications. Started coding at 16 with game development, evolved through mobile frameworks (Xamarin, Flutter), and now lead teams delivering enterprise software solutions.

**Army National Guard veteran** where I served as an Information Technology Specialist. Received the **Army Commendation Medal (ARCOM)** from the Adjutant General for developing a digital form submission system that streamlined armory sanitization across Oklahoma during COVID-19.

**B.S. Computer Science** — University of Central Oklahoma
